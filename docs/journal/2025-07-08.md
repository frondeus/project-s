Aaaah i think i know why!

In find_non_var we are only searching through upper bounds,
so we are searching for variables that are pointing towards our var.
But we need to also search lower bounds of other variables.

Ugh, actually that should not matter here. Because in that case what would happen,
is that we would miss "keyword" in record application and would get an extra error.
